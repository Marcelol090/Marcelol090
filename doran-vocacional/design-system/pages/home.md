# Home Page Override

## Composition

- Hero uses a 55/45 split on desktop and a single column on mobile.
- The headline remains concise and supported by clear explanatory copy.
- The RIASEC illustration is meaningful content and includes descriptive alt text.
- Four evidence metrics sit below the CTA to communicate scope without marketing inflation.

## Motion

- Only the illustration entry and two small route labels may move.
- No parallax, particle field, cursor follower or text scrambling.

## CTA hierarchy

1. Primary: begin or continue exploration.
2. Secondary: methodology.
3. Result navigation appears in the header only when the questionnaire is complete.
